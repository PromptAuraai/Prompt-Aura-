Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

Information We Collect

At PromptAura, we believe in minimal data collection. We only collect information that is necessary to provide and improve our services.

Information You Provide

  • Contact Information: When you contact us via email or contact form, we collect your name and email address.
  • Feedback: Any feedback, suggestions, or reviews you voluntarily provide.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, and features used (anonymized).

Cookies & Tracking GDPR Compliant

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and serve relevant advertisements through Google AdSense.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by Google AdSense to show personalized ads.

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

Google AdSense & Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on PromptAura. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

Third-Party Vendors

  • Google (AdSense) - Google Privacy Policy

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • To improve our website and user experience
  • To display relevant advertisements
  • To analyze website traffic and usage patterns

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services that help us operate our website (analytics, hosting).
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

PromptAura is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of personalized advertising

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@promptauraai.com.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@promptauraai.com
  • Through our Contact Page

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
