Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the prompts?
Simply browse our library, find a prompt you like, and click the "Copy Prompt" button. Then paste it into your favorite AI image generator like Midjourney, DALL-E, or Stable Diffusion.
Are the prompts free to use?
Yes! All prompts on PromptAura are completely free to use for personal and commercial projects. No credit card required, no hidden fees.
Can I contribute my own prompts?
We're always looking for new prompt contributions! Feel free to contact us with your prompt ideas, and we'll consider adding them to our library.
Do I need to create an account?
No account required! PromptAura is designed to be accessible to everyone. Just visit our website and start copying prompts instantly.
